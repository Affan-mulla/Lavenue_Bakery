Craftsmanship Story

A disciplined ritual from flour to finish.

  1. 01

    Slow Fermentation

    We rest every dough overnight for depth, digestibility, and refined flavor structure.

  2. 02

    Hand Lamination

    Butter and dough are folded with precision to create airy layers and crisp edges.

  3. 03

    Final Finish

    Each piece is glazed, dusted, or bruleed to order for peak texture and aroma.
